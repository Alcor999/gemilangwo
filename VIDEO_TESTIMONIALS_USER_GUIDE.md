## For Admin Users

### Access Video Management
1. **Go to**: Admin Dashboard ‚Üí Video Gallery
2. **URL**: `/admin/videos`

### Manage Package Videos

#### View Videos
- **URL**: `/admin/videos/package/{packageId}`
- Shows all videos for a specific package
- Drag to reorder videos
- Toggle active/inactive status

#### Add New Video
- **URL**: `/admin/videos/package/{packageId}/create`
- Click "Add Video" button on package video list
- **Steps**:
  1. Select video type (Upload or YouTube)
  2. For uploads: Choose MP4/AVI/MOV/MKV file (max 500MB)
  3. For YouTube: Paste full YouTube URL
  4. Add video title and description
  5. Upload custom thumbnail (optional)
  6. Enable/disable visibility
  7. Click "Add Video"

#### Edit Video
- Click "Edit" (pencil icon) on any video
- Update title, description, or type
- Replace video or thumbnail (leave empty to keep current)
- Update active status
- Click "Update Video"

#### Delete Video
- Click "Delete" (trash icon) on any video
- Confirm deletion in modal
- Video files are automatically removed

#### Reorder Videos
- Drag videos by the grip handle (‚â° icon)
- Drop in new position
- Order automatically saved

## For Customer Users

### Access Testimonials
1. **From Dashboard**: Click "My Testimonials" in sidebar
2. **URL**: `/customer/testimonials`
3. **From Homepage**: Click "Share Your Story" button

### My Testimonials

#### View My Testimonials
- **URL**: `/customer/testimonials`
- Shows all your video testimonials
- See status: Published, Pending Review, or Featured
- View count display
- Edit or delete any testimonial

#### Create New Testimonial
- **URL**: `/customer/testimonials/create`
- Click "Add Testimonial" button
- **Steps**:
  1. Enter testimonial title
  2. Write your wedding story (min. 10 characters)
  3. Select video type (Upload or YouTube)
  4. For uploads: Choose video file (MP4, AVI, MOV, MKV - max 500MB)
  5. For YouTube: Paste full YouTube URL
  6. Upload thumbnail image (recommended: 1280x720 or 1920x1080)
  7. Link to your wedding order (optional)
  8. Rate your experience (1-5 stars)
  9. Click "Submit Testimonial"
  
**Note**: Your testimonial will be reviewed by our team before publishing.

#### Edit Testimonial
- Click "Edit" button on your testimonial card
- Update any details
- Resubmits for review when you save
- Previous status resets to Pending Review

#### Delete Testimonial
- Click "Delete" button on your testimonial card
- Confirm deletion in modal
- Video and thumbnail files are automatically removed

## üé¨ Video Type Guide

### Upload Your Own Video
**Best For**: Custom wedding videos, personal testimonials
- **Supported Formats**: MP4, AVI, MOV, MKV
- **Max Size**: 500MB
- **Recommended Resolution**: 1920x1080 (Full HD) or 1280x720 (HD)
- **Recommended Length**: 30-90 seconds
- **Storage**: Stored on your server

### YouTube Link
**Best For**: Sharing existing YouTube videos
- **Steps**:
  1. Find video on YouTube
  2. Copy full URL from address bar
  3. Example: `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID`
  4. Paste into YouTube URL field
- **Advantages**: No storage used, high quality playback
- **Auto-Extract**: Thumbnail automatically shown

## üéØ Tips for Great Testimonials

### Before Recording
- Find a well-lit, quiet location
- Use a tripod or stable phone holder
- Test your camera and audio
- Have your message ready

### While Recording
- Look at the camera (not yourself)
- Speak clearly and naturally
- Smile and show genuine emotion
- Avoid background noise
- Maintain good posture

### After Recording
- Write a catchy title
- Include specific details about your wedding
- Mention what you loved most
- Keep description under 500 characters for website display
- Upload a clear thumbnail image

### Video Best Practices
- Keep it 30-90 seconds long
- Use good lighting (natural light is best)
- Clean background
- Professional or casual style
- Include your names or initials

## ‚ùì FAQ

### Q: What formats are supported?
**A**: MP4, AVI, MOV, MKV for uploads. YouTube links for embedded videos.

### Q: How long can my video be?
**A**: No technical limit, but 30-90 seconds is recommended for website.

### Q: Can I edit my testimonial after submitting?
**A**: Yes, click "Edit" on your testimonial. It will need to be reviewed again.

### Q: When will my testimonial be published?
**A**: Admin reviews submissions and approves them. Usually within 24-48 hours.

### Q: Can I delete my testimonial?
**A**: Yes, click "Delete" and confirm. It will be removed immediately.

### Q: Is my video stored securely?
**A**: Yes, uploaded videos are stored on our secure server with restricted access.

### Q: Can I use YouTube videos as testimonials?
**A**: Yes, simply paste the YouTube URL. No file upload needed.

### Q: What if I upload to YouTube instead?
**A**: Paste your YouTube URL in the testimonial form. We'll embed it on the website.
